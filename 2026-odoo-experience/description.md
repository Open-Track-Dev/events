Odoo Experience is the world's largest Open Source business software event, held annually in Brussels. Bringing together
thousands of developers, partners, and entrepreneurs, the 3-day conference features the exclusive unveiling of the
latest Odoo version. Attendees can expect over 350 insightful talks, hands-on workshops, and 'smart classes' covering
everything from technical development to business management. Beyond learning, it offers unparalleled networking
opportunities and legendary evening parties, making it the ultimate gathering for the Odoo community.
