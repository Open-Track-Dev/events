EndOf10, i.e. the end of hardware planned obsolescence, will be celebrated
with the help of FUSS GNU/Linux and lots of Free Software at the Linux Day
2025 - South Tyrol. Organized by the Linux User Group Bozen-Bolzano-Bulsan
(LUGBZ), the FUSS Project, the SwapToLearn-Helfergruppe (Vinzenzgemeinschaft),
PC-Doktor-Bozen and the Polo Culturale Terraferma.
